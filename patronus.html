What's Patronus?


The Patronus Charm (Expecto Patronum) was the most famous and nr one of the
most powerful defensive charms known to wizardkind.[4] It was an immensely
complicated and extremely difficult spell that channelled the caster's
positive emotions into a powerful protection and evoked a partially-tangible
positive energy force known as a Patronus (pl. Patronuses)or spirit guardian.
It was the primary protection against Dementors and Lethifolds, against which
there were no other defences.
